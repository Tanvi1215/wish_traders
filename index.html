About Wish Traders

Founded in 2023 in Nagpur, Maharashtra, Wish Traders is a fast-growing proprietorship firm specializing in the manufacturing and wholesale trading of premium paper-based products. We focus on delivering high-quality, eco-friendly, and customized packaging and branding solutions designed to meet modern business needs across various industries.

Our product range includes paper cups, paper wristbands, thermal paper rolls, pizza boxes, silicone wristbands, patient identification bands, and more — all crafted for durability, functionality, visual appeal, and sustainability. At Wish Traders, we believe packaging is more than protection; it's a brand statement. Driven by innovation and quality manufacturing, we aim to provide environmentally responsible solutions that elevate every brand we serve.
